about

I’m a visual designer that enjoys creating attractive, clean designs for web & mobile interfaces. I’ve worked with clients such as Google & Office Freedom; alongside law firms, e-commerce sites and even a pet store. I’m currently open to new positions, so feel free to get in touch.
